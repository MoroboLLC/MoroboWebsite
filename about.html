At 23 years old, Robert founded Morobo with a simple belief: great software should be accessible to everyone. What started as a solo venture has grown into a full-service software company delivering real products to real clients.

Robert travels full-time while building apps, designing websites, and developing AI solutions. Our Mission

We're on a mission to build innovative software that makes an impact. Every app we publish, every website we launch, and every AI agent we deploy is designed to solve real problems and deliver genuine value. We're a startup with big ambitions: to create a product so useful and well-crafted that it becomes part of everyday life.

What We Do

From concept to deployment, we handle every stage of the development process.

Mobile Apps

We design and develop innovative mobile applications for both iOS and Android. From consumer apps published under the Morobo brand to custom-built apps for clients, every project is crafted for performance and elegance.

Websites

Beautiful, responsive websites built for businesses of all sizes. Whether it's a portfolio, e-commerce platform, or landing page, we create web experiences that captivate visitors and convert them into customers.

AI Solutions

Intelligent AI agents that automate business operations. From AI receptionists and customer support bots to content generators, we build solutions that save time and drive growth for businesses.

Client Projects

We collaborate with entrepreneurs, digital marketers, and professionals around the world to bring their ideas to life. Every client project gets the same dedication and quality as our own products.

What Drives Us

Innovation First

We're not interested in building the same thing everyone else is building. We push boundaries, experiment with new technologies, and aim to create software that stands out in a crowded market.

Quality Over Quantity

Every project we take on gets our full attention. We'd rather deliver one exceptional product than ten mediocre ones. Details matter, and we obsess over getting them right.

Global Mindset

Software has no borders. We work with clients from anywhere in the world and build products designed for global audiences. Location is never a barrier to great collaboration.

Startup Hustle

We move fast, adapt quickly, and stay hungry. As a startup, we bring the energy and dedication that larger companies often lack. Every project is personal to us.
