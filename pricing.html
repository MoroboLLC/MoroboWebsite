Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the monthly fee?

Monthly fees cover hosting, security updates, technical support, regular backups, and minor content updates. For websites, this ensures your site stays fast, secure, and up-to-date.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to a higher tier at any time. We'll work with you to migrate your existing setup and add the new features you need.

How long does development take?

Websites typically take 2-4 weeks, mobile apps take 8-16 weeks, and AI automation projects vary based on complexity. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Yes! If our standard packages don't fit your needs, we can create a custom solution tailored to your specific requirements and budget.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and can set up payment plans for larger projects. Contact us to discuss payment options.

Do you provide training?

Yes! All packages include training on how to use and manage your website, app, or AI system. We provide documentation and ongoing support.
