Getting Started

Welcome to Pregamify! Here's how to get started:

  1. Download the App: Get Pregamify from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. No Account Needed: Open the app and start training immediately — no sign-up required.
  3. Pick a Category: Choose from Reaction, Tracking, Visual, or Precision to focus your warmup.
  4. Play a Minigame: Each category has five minigames. Start with any of them.
  5. Track Progress: Your personal bests and streaks save locally — just come back before your next gaming session.

Training Categories

Pregamify covers four core gaming senses, each with five minigames:

  • ⚡ Reaction: Pure response speed. Train the gap between seeing something and acting on it.
  • 🎯 Tracking: Smooth target following — critical for shooters, sports games, and anything with fast motion.
  • 👁️ Visual: Pattern recognition, peripheral awareness, and quick visual parsing.
  • 🎮 Precision: Fine motor control, flick accuracy, and micro-adjustments under pressure.

Try the Daily Challenge for a rotating mix that hits all four categories in a single short session.

Pregamify Pro

Pro is an optional subscription that unlocks the full experience:

  • Ad-Free: Zero ads while your subscription is active.
  • All Minigames: Access every minigame across all four categories.
  • Higher Difficulty: Advanced tiers for serious training.
  • Endless Mode: Unlimited reps — keep going as long as you want.

Managing your subscription: Subscriptions are billed through Apple. To cancel, go to iOS Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions. You'll keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period. Refund requests go through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Progress & Scores

  • Local Storage Only: All your scores, streaks, and session history are stored on your device, not in the cloud.
  • No Cross-Device Sync: Currently scores don't transfer between devices. Uninstalling the app or resetting your device will clear them.
  • Reset Progress: Settings inside the app let you clear your history if you want a fresh start.
  • Privacy: Because nothing leaves your device, we never see your scores or play patterns.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will Pregamify really make me better at my game?

Pregamify trains the underlying senses — reaction, tracking, visual processing, and precision — that most games rely on. It's designed as a warmup tool. It's not a medical or therapeutic product, and we make no guarantees about specific in-game performance. Treat it like a stretching routine for your hands and eyes.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Pregamify is fully account-less. Just download, open, and start.

What's the difference between free and Pro?

Free users get a subset of minigames with ads between sessions. Pro unlocks every minigame, all difficulty tiers, Endless mode, and removes ads entirely.

Why do ads show up sometimes?

Ads let us offer Pregamify free to everyone. Subscribe to Pro if you'd prefer an uninterrupted experience.

How do I cancel Pro?

Go to iOS Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions and cancel Pregamify Pro. Pro access stays active until the end of your current billing period.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No scores, personal bests, or progress ever leave your device. See our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email support@morobo.org with a description, your device model, and iOS version. Screenshots or screen recordings help a lot.
