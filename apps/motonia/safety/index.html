Safety Guidance

Motonia - ride safe, always

Last Updated: 2026-02-26

1. Eyes on the Road

Do not interact with Motonia while riding. Pull over safely before touching your device.

2. Mount and Audio Setup

  • Use a secure mount and keep the device outside your direct line of sight.
  • Prefer audio guidance where legal and safe.

3. Treat App Data as Assistive

Navigation, live rider, and hazard information may be delayed or inaccurate. Always follow real-world conditions, traffic controls, and local law.

4. Hazard Reporting

Only report hazards when stopped and only if you can do so safely. False reports are prohibited.

5. Emergency Situations

If there is immediate danger or an emergency, contact local emergency services first.

6. Contact

Safety questions: contact@morobo.org
