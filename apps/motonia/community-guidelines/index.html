Community Guidelines

Motonia - Respectful and safety-first rider community standards

Last Updated: 2026-02-26

1. Be Respectful

Harassment, hate speech, threats, or abusive behavior is not allowed.

2. Report Honestly

Hazard reports must be accurate and submitted in good faith. False or spam reports may result in account action.

3. Do Not Distract Yourself While Riding

Do not interact with Motonia while riding. Pull over safely before interacting with app features.

4. No Illegal or Harmful Content

Do not upload or share illegal, dangerous, or infringing content.

5. Enforcement

Morobo LLC may remove content, limit visibility, suspend, or terminate accounts that violate these guidelines or the Terms of Service.

6. Contact

Questions or appeals: contact@morobo.org
