Current Release Scope

Motonia currently includes:

  • Motorcycle navigation
  • Live rider visibility on map
  • Hazard reporting and hazard display
  • Account and profile features

Not currently active in this release: proximity chat and group rides.

Contact & Response Times

For support, legal, or account requests, email:

contact@morobo.org

  • General support: typically within 2-3 business days
  • Account/security issues: typically within 1 business day
  • Legal/privacy requests: typically within 7 business days

Accounts, Deletion, and Legal Requests

To request account deletion or privacy/legal actions, email contact@morobo.org with:

  • The email address used for your account
  • Request type (deletion, data access, correction, etc.)
  • Any relevant details to verify ownership

We process deletion and privacy requests in accordance with our Privacy Policy and applicable law.
