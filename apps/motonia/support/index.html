FAQ

When will Motonia launch?

We're targeting a beta release in early 2026, with a full public launch later in the year. Sign up for notifications to be the first to know!

Will it be free?

Motonia will offer a free version with core navigation features. Premium features like offline maps, advanced route planning, and community features will be available through subscription.

What devices will be supported?

Motonia will be available for both iOS (iPhone) and Android smartphones. We're also exploring integration with motorcycle-specific GPS units.

Can I suggest features?

Absolutely! We'd love to hear from riders about what features matter most to you. Contact us with your ideas!
