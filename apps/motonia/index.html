What it does

Built for
the open road.

🏍️

Motorcycle-Optimized Routes

Navigation designed for two wheels — prioritizing curves, scenic roads, and avoiding unnecessary highways.

🗺️

Offline Maps

Download maps before you ride so you're never lost, even without cell service in remote areas.

🌄

Scenic Route Discovery

Find the most beautiful and enjoyable roads in your area or while traveling to new places.

⚠️

Safety Alerts

Real-time alerts for road hazards, sharp turns, weather conditions, and construction zones.

📍

Route Sharing

Share your favorite rides with friends and the Motonia riding community.

🌤️

Weather Integration

Check conditions along your entire route before you even put on your helmet.

📊

Ride Statistics

Track your miles, favorite routes, and riding achievements over time.

👥

Group Rides

Coordinate group rides with friends, share live locations, and meet up on the road.
