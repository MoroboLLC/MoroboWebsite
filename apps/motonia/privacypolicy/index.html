Privacy Policy

Motonia App

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

Introduction

Morobo LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Motonia mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our motorcycle GPS navigation app.

By using Motonia, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Location Data

As a GPS navigation app, Motonia requires access to your location to function properly:

  • Real-time GPS location for navigation
  • Route history and tracking data
  • Location-based services (weather, hazard alerts)

You can control location permissions in your device settings. Disabling location will limit app functionality.

Personal Information

We may collect:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Name and profile information (optional)
  • Payment information (processed by App Store/Google Play)

Usage Data

  • Routes created and saved
  • Navigation preferences
  • App settings and customizations
  • Device information and performance data

How We Use Your Information

Navigation Services

  • Provide turn-by-turn navigation
  • Calculate optimal motorcycle routes
  • Display real-time traffic and road conditions
  • Offer offline map functionality

Safety Features

  • Weather alerts along your route
  • Road hazard warnings
  • Emergency contact notifications (if enabled)

Community Features

  • Share routes with other riders
  • Group ride coordination
  • Discover popular riding routes

Location Data Privacy

We take your location privacy seriously:

  • Location data is used only for navigation and app features
  • You control what route data is shared with the community
  • Historical location data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not sell your location data to third parties
  • You can delete your location history at any time

Data Sharing

Public Sharing (Optional)

When you choose to share routes publicly:

  • Route paths and waypoints become visible to other users
  • Your username may be associated with shared routes
  • You can delete or make routes private at any time

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for:

  • Map data and navigation (OpenStreetMap, Google Maps)
  • Weather information
  • Analytics (anonymized)
  • Crash reporting

Offline Maps

Downloaded offline maps are stored locally on your device and do not require an internet connection. We do not track your location when using offline maps unless you explicitly enable features that require connectivity.

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Route history: Stored locally on your device, synced to cloud if enabled
  • Usage analytics: Anonymized and aggregated, retained for 24 months

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your route data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Control location permissions
  • Opt-out of data sharing and analytics
  • Control what routes are public or private

Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Device-level data encryption

Children's Privacy

Motonia is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email.
