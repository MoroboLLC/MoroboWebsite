Privacy Policy

Motonia - Motorcycle GPS App

Last Updated: 2026-06-17

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Morobo LLC ("Morobo," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use Motonia.

Motonia is intended for adults aged 18 and older. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Identity

  • Username, email address, and profile image
  • Authentication and account security records

2.2 Precise Location Data

  • Real-time GPS location for navigation, free-ride/Explore, and live-rider map features
  • Location used to determine proximity voice range and group ride positioning
  • Location context used for hazard and route relevance

2.3 Microphone / Voice Audio

  • When you turn on proximity voice chat or join a group voice chat, your microphone is used to transmit live audio to other participants.
  • We do NOT record, store, or sell your voice audio — it is streamed in real time through our voice provider and then gone.

2.4 Ride and Safety Data

  • Routes, trip stats, and ride history
  • Top speed and other ride telemetry shown on community leaderboards
  • Hazard and police reports submitted by users
  • Moderation reports, blocks, and enforcement records

2.5 Diagnostics and Analytics

  • Crash logs, performance diagnostics, and app usage analytics
  • Device/app metadata necessary to improve reliability and security

3. How We Use Data

  • Navigation and ride modes: Routing, free-ride, and Explore functionality
  • Safety: Hazard and police awareness and safety-related map context
  • Live-rider visibility and voice: Show riders and enable proximity/group voice according to your settings
  • Community leaderboards: Display ride and top-speed standings from self-reported telemetry
  • Account and security: Authentication, abuse prevention, and enforcement
  • Reliability: Debugging, performance monitoring, and service improvement

4. Information Shared With Other Users

When you enable live-rider visibility or join a proximity or group session, other users may see your approximate real-time location, username, profile image, motorcycle, movement, and (in voice sessions) hear your live voice. You control this with the visibility setting (All / Friends / None) and by leaving sessions. Hazard and police reports you submit are shared with nearby users.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information.

  • Service providers/processors: We share data only as needed to operate Motonia, including Mapbox (maps/navigation), Supabase (backend/auth/data), and LiveKit (real-time voice transport).
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose data when required by law, court order, or to protect safety, rights, or property.
  • Business operations: Limited, secure disclosure for audits, security response, or lawful corporate transactions.

6. User Controls

  • Location and microphone permissions: You can grant or revoke precise location and microphone access in your device settings.
  • Live-rider visibility: You can choose visibility options such as All, Friends, or None.
  • Voice chat: You can turn proximity/group voice on or off, and mute or block other users.
  • Reporting: You can report users or content (including from the live-rider card).
  • Account deletion: You can request account deletion and associated personal data removal via contact@morobo.org.

7. Data Retention

  • Account records: Retained while account is active; deleted or anonymized within 30 days after verified deletion request, except where legally required.
  • Ride, safety, and leaderboard records: Retained for up to 24 months to support user history, safety analysis, and abuse prevention; then deleted or aggregated.
  • Moderation and security logs: Retained up to 36 months for trust and safety enforcement.
  • Diagnostics: Retained up to 12 months unless needed longer for ongoing incident investigations.
  • Live voice audio: Not recorded and not retained.

8. Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS)
  • Role-based access controls and least-privilege access
  • Operational monitoring and incident response procedures
  • Security review processes for infrastructure and application changes

9. Children

Motonia is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

10. Your Rights and Requests

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data. To make a privacy or account deletion request, email contact@morobo.org.
