Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Morobo ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Thriftaholic mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App. Please read this policy carefully. By accessing or using the App you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account we collect your email address, username, and optional profile details such as a profile photo, clothing sizes, style preferences, and favorite brands. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive limited profile information (name, email) from the identity provider; we do not receive your password.

1.2 User-Generated Content

When you create a post we collect the photographs you upload, the item description, category, price, and the store location you associate with the post. This content is stored on our servers and displayed publicly to other users of the App.

1.3 Location Data

With your permission we collect your device's precise geographic location (latitude and longitude) to display nearby thrift stores and to show you relevant posts. Location is obtained through the device's GPS, Wi-Fi, or cellular network. You may revoke location permission at any time through your device settings; however, some features of the App will not function without it.

1.4 Camera and Photo Library

With your permission we access your device camera to let you take photos of items to post and to scan items using our AI Scanner feature. We also access your photo library to let you select existing photos for posts or your profile picture. Photos used with the AI Scanner are transmitted to our server for processing and are not retained after the analysis is complete.

1.5 Device Information and Identifiers

We automatically collect certain device information including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and, where applicable and with your consent, the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) for the purpose of serving personalized ads.

1.6 Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with the App, including pages and features accessed, posts viewed, likes, searches, time spent on screens, and crash reports. This data helps us improve the App and diagnose technical issues.

1.7 Push Notification Tokens

If you opt in to push notifications we collect your device push token to send you alerts about wishlist matches, community activity, and other updates. You may disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.

1.8 Payment and Subscription Data

Premium subscriptions are processed and managed entirely by Apple through In-App Purchases. We do not collect or store your credit card number or payment credentials. We receive a confirmation of your subscription status (active, expired, or cancelled) from our subscription management partner (RevenueCat) to unlock Premium features.

1.9 AI Scanner Data

When you use the AI Scanner feature, the photograph you submit is sent to our server-side function, which forwards a processed (resized) version to OpenAI's API for analysis. A web search query may also be generated and sent to the Brave Search API to obtain resale pricing data. We do not retain the image or search results after the analysis response is returned to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the App — display nearby stores, show posts, and enable core features.
  • Personalize your experience — tailor content, recommendations, and search results based on your preferences and location.
  • Process subscriptions — verify your Premium status and unlock associated features.
  • Send notifications — alert you to wishlist matches, new posts at favorite stores, and account activity (with your consent).
  • AI-powered item analysis — process photos through our AI Scanner to identify items and estimate resale value.
  • Serve advertisements — display ads through Google AdMob to non-Premium users (see Section 5).
  • Improve and develop features — analyze aggregated usage data to optimize performance, fix bugs, and build new functionality.
  • Ensure safety and enforce policies — detect fraud, abuse, spam, and violations of our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  • Comply with legal obligations — respond to lawful requests from governmental authorities.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Public Content

Posts you create (including photos, descriptions, prices, and associated store) are visible to all App users. Your username and profile photo are displayed alongside your posts.

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App, subject to confidentiality obligations:

  • Supabase — cloud database hosting, authentication, and file storage (servers located in the United States).
  • Google Maps Platform — map display and store location rendering.
  • Geoapify — nearby thrift store discovery (receives your approximate location to return store listings).
  • Google AdMob — advertisement serving for non-Premium users (may collect device identifiers and usage data; see Section 5).
  • RevenueCat — subscription status management (receives your anonymous user identifier and subscription receipt).
  • OpenAI — AI-powered item identification for the Scanner feature (receives processed item photographs; see Section 1.9).
  • Brave Search API — web search queries for resale pricing in the Scanner feature (receives a text search query only, no images or personal data).
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — delivery of push notifications.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via the App or email of any change in ownership or use of your personal information.

4. Cookies, Local Storage, and Similar Technologies

The App uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to store your preferences (such as dark mode, onboarding status, and cached location). This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers except as part of normal App functionality. Third-party advertising SDKs may use their own tracking technologies as described in Section 5.

5. Advertising and Tracking

Non-Premium users see advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers (including IDFA, where consented), IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or contextual ads. On iOS, we request your consent through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before any cross-app tracking occurs. You may opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking — disable tracking for Thriftaholic.
  • Ad settings: Visit adssettings.google.com to manage Google ad personalization.
  • Upgrade to Premium: Premium subscribers see no third-party ads.

6. Data Retention

  • Posts: Posts remain visible in the public feed and store pages for 30 days after creation. After 30 days posts are no longer displayed publicly but remain accessible on the author's own profile. Posts that are manually or automatically soft-deleted are permanently erased (including associated images) after an additional 30-day grace period.
  • Account data: Your account information and profile are retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your profile, posts, likes, favorites, wishlist, and associated images. Some data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 30 days after deletion.
  • AI Scanner data: Photos and results from the AI Scanner are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers after the response is delivered.
  • Usage and analytics data: Aggregated and de-identified usage data may be retained indefinitely for product improvement purposes.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth with support for OAuth providers (Apple, Google).
  • Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on our database to ensure users can only access authorized data.
  • Regular review of access controls and service provider security practices.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially reasonable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

8. Your Privacy Rights

8.1 All Users

Regardless of your location you may:

  • Access your personal data by viewing your profile and posts in the App.
  • Correct inaccurate data by editing your profile.
  • Delete your account and associated data through the App (Profile > Settings > Delete Account).
  • Opt out of push notifications and location access through your device settings.
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us at the email below.

8.2 California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act:

  • Right to Know: You may request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising except through AdMob as described in Section 5, which you may opt out of via ATT or device settings.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA/CPRA rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@morobo.org. We will verify your identity before fulfilling any request. You may designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf.

8.3 European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland:

  • Our legal basis for processing your personal data includes: your consent (e.g., location, push notifications, ATT tracking), performance of a contract (providing the App), and our legitimate interests (improving the App, preventing abuse).
  • You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and port your data.
  • You have the right to withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
  • You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
  • Data transfers outside the EEA are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards adopted by our service providers.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States, where our servers and service providers are located. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your jurisdiction. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your information remains protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@morobo.org.

11. Third-Party Links and Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as store websites, Google Maps directions, or phone dialer). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@morobo.org
  • General inquiries: contact@morobo.org
  • Help: help@morobo.org
