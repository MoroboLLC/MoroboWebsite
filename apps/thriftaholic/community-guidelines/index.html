Community Guidelines

Effective Date: April 7, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 7, 2026

Thriftaholic is a community built on sharing amazing thrift-store finds. These guidelines help keep our community positive, helpful, and safe for everyone. By using the App you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban.

1. Be Authentic

  • Only post items you have actually seen at a thrift store, consignment shop, or similar secondhand retailer.
  • Provide accurate descriptions, categories, and prices.
  • Mark items as sold promptly when they are no longer available.
  • Do not post items from regular retail stores, online shops, or items you are personally selling.

2. Be Respectful

  • Treat all community members with kindness and respect.
  • Celebrate others' finds — thrift culture is about sharing the excitement.
  • Disagreements are fine; personal attacks are not.
  • Do not discriminate based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or age.

3. Keep It Safe

  • Do not share personal information of others (phone numbers, addresses, full names) without their consent.
  • Do not use the App to stalk, harass, or intimidate anyone.
  • Do not encourage or glorify self-harm, violence, or illegal activity.
  • If you feel unsafe because of another user's behavior, report it immediately and contact local authorities if necessary.

4. Content Standards

The following content is prohibited and will be removed:

  • Hate speech and discrimination — content that attacks individuals or groups based on protected characteristics.
  • Harassment and bullying — repeated unwanted contact, threats, or intimidation.
  • Sexually explicit material — nudity, pornography, or sexually suggestive content.
  • Violence and graphic content — content depicting or promoting violence, gore, or harm.
  • Illegal items and activity — stolen goods, counterfeit products, controlled substances, weapons (where prohibited), or any other illegal items.
  • Spam and misleading content — repetitive posts, clickbait, scams, or deceptive information.
  • Personal information (doxxing) — sharing someone's private information without consent.
  • Copyright infringement — content that infringes on another party's intellectual property rights.
  • Impersonation — pretending to be another person, brand, or entity.
  • Misinformation — deliberately false claims about items, stores, or other users.

5. AI Scanner Use

  • Use the AI Scanner for its intended purpose: identifying and valuing thrift-store items.
  • Do not submit inappropriate, offensive, or illegal images to the Scanner.
  • Scanner results are estimates — do not misrepresent AI valuations as professional appraisals.

6. Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please report it:

  • Tap on any post, open the menu (three dots icon), and select "Report."
  • Choose the appropriate reason and provide any helpful details.
  • You may also email reports directly to support@morobo.org.

All reports are reviewed by our moderation team. We aim to review reports within 24 hours. Reports are confidential — the reported user will not be told who submitted the report.

7. Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in one or more of the following actions, at our sole discretion:

  • Content removal — the offending post or content is deleted.
  • Warning — a notification that your content or behavior violated our guidelines.
  • Temporary suspension — your account is temporarily restricted from posting or interacting.
  • Permanent ban — your account is permanently disabled for serious or repeated violations.

We reserve the right to take action on any content or account that we determine, in our sole discretion, violates the spirit of these guidelines, even if not explicitly covered above.

8. Appeals

If you believe your content was removed or your account was actioned in error, you may appeal by contacting support@morobo.org with the subject line "Appeal." Include your username and a description of the situation. We will review all appeals and respond within 7 business days.

9. Changes to These Guidelines

We may update these Community Guidelines from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated guidelines.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about these Community Guidelines, please contact us:

  • Email: support@morobo.org
  • General inquiries: contact@morobo.org
  • Help: help@morobo.org
