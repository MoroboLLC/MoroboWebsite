Getting Started

Welcome to AgentBoard! Here's how to get started with your AI agent analytics dashboard:

  1. Create an Account: Sign up with your email address to create your AgentBoard account.
  2. Connect Your AI Platform: Link your Retell AI, Vapi, Synthflow, or custom webhook provider.
  3. Configure Webhooks: Set up webhooks to receive real-time call data from your AI agents.
  4. View Your Dashboard: Start monitoring your agent performance with real-time metrics.
  5. Invite Clients: Share access with your clients so they can view their specific agent data.

Supported Integrations

AgentBoard supports the following AI voice agent platforms:

  • Retell AI: Full integration with call metadata, transcripts, and outcomes.
  • Vapi: Connect your Vapi agents for comprehensive analytics.
  • Synthflow: Track Synthflow agent performance and metrics.
  • Custom Webhooks: Use our webhook API to connect any AI agent platform.

Each integration provides access to call duration, outcomes, sentiment analysis, and custom metadata.

Client Access

AgentBoard allows you to invite clients to view their specific agent analytics:

  • Client Invitations: Send email invitations to your clients to access their dashboard.
  • Filtered Views: Clients only see data for their specific agents and campaigns.
  • Branded Experience: Customize the client portal with your agency branding.
  • PDF Reports: Clients can generate and download PDF reports of their metrics.

Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms does AgentBoard support?

AgentBoard currently supports Retell AI, Vapi, Synthflow, and any platform that can send webhook data. We're continuously adding new integrations.

Is my data secure?

Yes! AgentBoard uses industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. We use Supabase for secure backend infrastructure with row-level security.

Can I use AgentBoard on multiple devices?

Yes, your AgentBoard account works across iOS, Android, and web. Your data syncs automatically across all devices.

How many clients can I invite?

The number of clients depends on your subscription plan. Free plans include limited client access, while paid plans offer unlimited client invitations.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your app store account settings (Apple App Store or Google Play Store).
