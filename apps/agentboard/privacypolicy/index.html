Privacy Policy

AgentBoard - AI Agent Analytics Dashboard

Effective Date: February 2025 | Last Updated: February 2025

1. Introduction

MoroboLLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates AgentBoard (the "Service," "App," or "Platform"), a software-as-a-service dashboard for AI voice agent analytics.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our Service. This policy applies to all users of the Service, including agency administrators and their invited clients.

By using AgentBoard, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly, information collected automatically, and information from third-party integrations.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored in encrypted/hashed form)
  • Business/agency name
  • Profile information

Client Information:

  • Names of clients you add to the platform
  • Client email addresses (if provided)
  • Client-specific settings and configurations

Payment Information:

  • Billing name and address
  • Payment method details (processed and stored by our payment processors: RevenueCat, Apple, Google; we do not store full payment card numbers)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Data:

  • Features accessed and actions taken within the App
  • Date and time of access
  • Time spent on pages/screens
  • Click patterns and navigation paths

Device Information:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information
  • IP address

2.3 Information from Third-Party Integrations

AI Agent Platform Data:

When you connect third-party AI agent platforms (such as Retell AI, Vapi, Synthflow, or custom systems) to AgentBoard, we receive and process data sent via webhooks, which may include:

  • Call metadata (call ID, timestamps, duration)
  • Call outcomes (answered, voicemail, transferred, etc.)
  • Call transcripts
  • Sentiment analysis results
  • Lead and appointment indicators
  • Cost/usage metrics
  • Custom fields and metadata

Important: We receive this data from third-party platforms. The collection and transmission of this data to us is governed by your agreement with those platforms. You are responsible for ensuring you have appropriate rights and consents to share this data with AgentBoard.

  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues

Service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

If you are an agency, your designated clients can access their specific analytics data through the Service. You control which clients have access and what data they can see.

We retain your personal information for as long as:

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information.

7.1 Rights for All Users

All users may:
