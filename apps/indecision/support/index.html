Getting Started

Welcome to Indecision! Here's how to get started making better decisions:

  1. Download the app from the Apple App Store
  2. Create your first decision by tapping the "+" button
  3. Enter your options and any relevant details
  4. Let AI analyze your decision and provide recommendations
  5. Review the insights and make your choice with confidence

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI analysis work?

Our AI uses advanced natural language processing to understand your decision context, analyze the options you provide, and generate insights based on common decision-making frameworks and best practices.

Is my data private?

Yes! We take your privacy seriously. Your decisions are stored locally on your device and encrypted. We never share your personal decision data with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use the app offline?

You can view your past decisions offline, but AI analysis requires an internet connection to provide recommendations.

When is the Android version coming?

We're actively working on the Android version! Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when it launches.

How much does it cost?

Indecision offers a free tier with basic features. Premium features including unlimited AI analyses and advanced insights are available through subscription.

Troubleshooting

The app won't load

Try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Force close and restart the app
  • Update to the latest version
  • Restart your device

AI analysis isn't working

Ensure you have an active internet connection and that you haven't reached your analysis limit. Premium users have unlimited analyses.

I can't restore my purchase

Go to Settings > Account > Restore Purchases. If issues persist, contact support with your receipt.
