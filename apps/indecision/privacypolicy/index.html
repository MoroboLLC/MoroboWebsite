Privacy Policy

Indecision App

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

Introduction

Morobo LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Indecision mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Indecision, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not download or use the App.

What We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for account recovery and communication)
  • Account credentials (encrypted password)
  • Account settings and preferences

Note: No email verification is required. Account creation is simple and immediate.

Decision Input Data

When you use Indecision, you provide decision information through:

  • Text input: Decisions you type into the app
  • Voice input: Decisions you speak (processed using Apple's built-in speech recognition on your device)

Important: Your decision text is sent to our AI provider for analysis. While we anonymize this data, please avoid including highly sensitive personal information in your decisions (like passwords, financial account numbers, etc.).

Usage Statistics

We automatically collect anonymized usage data to improve the app:

  • Number of decisions analyzed
  • Features used (text vs. voice input)
  • Time spent in the app
  • App crashes and errors (for bug fixing)
  • Device type and operating system version

This data is not linked to your personal identity and is used only for improving app performance.

How We Use Your Information

AI Decision Analysis

When you submit a decision for analysis:

  • Your decision text is sent to our AI provider's servers
  • The AI analyzes your options and provides recommendations
  • Your decision content is not permanently stored by us or the AI provider
  • Processing happens in real-time and data is deleted after the response is generated
  • We do not use your decisions to train AI models

Account Management

We use your email to:

  • Send you account-related notifications
  • Provide customer support
  • Notify you of important updates or security issues
  • Process password changes and account recovery

App Improvements

We use aggregated, anonymized statistics to:

  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Understand which features are most valuable
  • Improve AI accuracy and speed
  • Enhance user experience

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Lives

  • On your device: Decision history is stored locally on your iPhone
  • On our servers: Only your email and account settings
  • AI processing: Temporarily during analysis, then immediately deleted

Security Measures

We protect your information through:

  • Encrypted HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Hashed and salted password storage
  • Local device encryption (via Apple's iOS security)
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure server infrastructure

Disclaimer: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

Third-Party Services

Indecision uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Provider: For processing decision analysis (data is not stored or used for training)
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and in-app purchases
  • Apple Speech Recognition: For voice-to-text conversion (processed on your device)
  • Analytics Service: For anonymized app usage statistics
  • Crash Reporting: For identifying and fixing bugs

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

What We Don't Do

To be absolutely clear:

  • ❌ We do NOT sell your data to anyone
  • ❌ We do NOT share your decisions with third parties (except AI processing)
  • ❌ We do NOT use your decisions to train AI models
  • ❌ We do NOT send you marketing emails (unless you opt in)
  • ❌ We do NOT track you across other apps or websites
  • ❌ We do NOT require email verification to use the app

Your Rights and Controls

Account Management

You can manage your account through the app settings:

  • Change password: Update your account password anytime
  • Delete account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Export data: Download a copy of your decision history
  • Clear history: Delete your local decision history

Privacy Controls

You can control privacy settings:

  • Disable analytics tracking
  • Disable crash reporting
  • Choose between text and voice input
  • Opt out of email notifications

Data Deletion

When you delete your account:

  • Your email and account data are permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
  • Local decision history is immediately deleted from your device
  • Anonymized usage statistics cannot be deleted (as they're not linked to you)
  • This action is irreversible

Subscription and Payments

Indecision offers in-app purchases through Apple's App Store:

  • All payments are processed by Apple
  • We do NOT store credit card information
  • Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID
  • Refunds must be requested through Apple

Apple's privacy policy applies to payment data. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

Children's Privacy

Indecision is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@morobo.com and we will delete it immediately.

International Users

If you use Indecision from outside the United States:

  • Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • U.S. data protection laws may differ from your country
  • By using the app, you consent to this transfer

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@morobo.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the app
  • Your continued use constitutes acceptance of changes

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests about this Privacy Policy:

  • Email: privacy@morobo.com
  • Support: Visit our Support Page
  • Mail: Morobo LLC, Fort Worth, Texas, USA

We typically respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
