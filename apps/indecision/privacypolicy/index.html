Privacy Policy

Indecision App

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

Introduction

Morobo LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Indecision mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Indecision, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not download or use the App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique identifiers)
  • App usage statistics (features used, time spent)
  • Crash reports and performance data

Decision Data

The decisions you create within the App are stored locally on your device and encrypted. We do not access or store your personal decision content on our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync data for backup purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the App
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features
  • Communicating with you about updates, security alerts, and support
  • Processing AI analysis requests
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security

AI Processing

When you request AI analysis of a decision, we send anonymized decision data to our AI processing servers. This data is:

  • Processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Stripped of personally identifiable information
  • Used solely to generate decision insights
  • Deleted immediately after processing

Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for decision data
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Local storage with device-level encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (anonymized usage data)
  • Crash reporting
  • Payment processing (App Store)
  • AI processing infrastructure

These services have their own privacy policies and we recommend reviewing them.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • In connection with a business transaction (merger, acquisition)

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your decision data
  • Disable analytics tracking in settings

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@morobo.com
  • Address: Morobo LLC, Fort Worth, Texas, USA
