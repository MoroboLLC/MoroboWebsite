Terms of Service — Master Services Agreement

Scope: This document governs the relationship between Morobo ("Provider") and any client ("Client") for website development, app development, AI agent creation, hosting, integrations, and dashboard services. Use this as the primary contract and attach a signed SOW for project specifics.

  • Services and deliverables definition
  • Client responsibilities and approvals
  • Change request process and fees
  • Payment schedule, deposits, milestones
  • Hosting, maintenance, and monitoring terms
  • Data handling and security obligations
  • Termination rights and post-termination obligations
  • Governing law and arbitration clauses

Note: Always attach a project-specific SOW and an Order Form to this agreement.

Privacy Policy & Data Handling

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal data for services including websites, apps, and AI agents. It covers user data, account data, payment metadata and analytics.

  • Categories of data collected (contact, account, payment, logs)
  • Purposes of processing and legal bases
  • Third-party processors and integrations (Stripe, Supabase, analytics)
  • User rights (access, correction, deletion, portability)
  • Data retention and deletion policy
  • Security measures and incident response
  • International transfers and safeguards

We recommend combining this with a DPA for enterprise customers and a clear cookie banner for websites that use tracking.

Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Use this addendum when processing personal data on behalf of a client. Includes subprocessors list, security requirements, breach notification timelines (72 hours), and audit rights.

  1. Nature and purpose of processing
  2. Duration of processing and categories of data
  3. Subprocessors and change notification
  4. Security measures and certifications
  5. Data subject rights assistance
  6. Return or deletion of data on termination

End-User License Agreement (EULA)

For delivered software (mobile apps or desktop tools) that will be redistributed to users. Covers license grant, restrictions, updates, support, and termination.

Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Define uptime guarantees, maintenance windows, response times for incidents, and credits on service-level lapses. Useful for hosting and dashboard customers on paid tiers.

Statement of Work (SOW) & Order Form

Template SOW items to include:

  • Project summary and objectives
  • Deliverables and acceptance criteria
  • Timeline and milestones
  • Payment schedule and fees
  • Resources and client responsibilities
  • Change control and scope creep process

Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Mutual and unilateral NDA templates for pre-sales conversations, discovery calls, and sensitive integrations. Include definitions of Confidential Information, permitted disclosures, duration, and exclusions.

Acceptable Use, Security & Compliance

Policies that govern how customers may (and may not) use the Services, plus security obligations:

  • Prohibited content and activities (illegal, infringing, abusive)
  • Account security responsibilities and MFA recommendation
  • Incident reporting and cooperation
  • Access control and least-privilege guidance

Payments, Billing & Refunds

Standard payment terms, accepted payment methods (Stripe etc.), overdue payments, suspension policy, refunds and chargebacks handling, and tax responsibilities.

Intellectual Property & Licensing

Who owns code, design assets, data and third-party components. Clauses for:

  • Work-for-hire vs. license grants
  • Third-party libraries & open-source dependencies
  • Transfer of ownership on full payment (if applicable)
  • Template language for client IP assignment

Liability, Indemnity & Dispute Resolution

Standard limitations of liability, indemnification for IP infringement, and dispute resolution terms (arbitration, venue, governing law).
