MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Morobo LLC ("Morobo") and Client

1. SERVICES OVERVIEW

Morobo will provide development and hosting services for websites, mobile apps, and AI agents.

2. PRICING TIERS & SCOPE LIMITS

WEBSITES: Basic (5 pages), Pro (10 pages), Enterprise (15+ pages)

MOBILE APPS: Basic (single platform), Pro (multi-platform), Enterprise (complex integrations)

AI AGENTS: Basic, Pro, Enterprise tiers based on complexity

3. SCOPE CREEP & UPGRADE POLICY

Clients requesting features beyond their tier scope will be charged for upgrade to next tier. Morobo retains discretion.

4. PAYMENT TERMS

Website & App Development: 50% deposit upon contract, 50% upon completion. Alternative split payments available.

AI Agents: First month fee as retainer BEFORE development begins.

Hosting & Maintenance: Monthly subscriptions - Basic ($49/month), Advanced ($79/month), Pro ($99/month)

All prices exclusive of tax. Client responsible for tax obligations.

5. NON-REFUNDABLE POLICY

All fees are non-refundable after work commencement. Deposits non-refundable if client cancels.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY OWNERSHIP

Morobo retains 100% ownership of all code, design, architecture. Client receives NON-EXCLUSIVE LICENSE for contracted term only.

Upon termination/non-payment, all access is revoked immediately. Third-party components remain under original licenses.

7. HOSTING & DOMAIN OWNERSHIP

Domains: Morobo owns all domains. Client receives usage license for contracted term. Upon termination, domain reverts to Morobo.

Apps: Morobo owns app store accounts. Apps published under Morobo accounts. Upon termination, app delisted within 14 days.

8. UPTIME & PERFORMANCE

Uptime: 99% monthly availability. Scheduled maintenance: Sundays 2am‚Äì4am UTC.

Support Response: Basic (48 hours), Pro (4 hours), Enterprise (1 hour, 24/7)

9. CHANGES & REVISIONS

Included: Basic (1/month), Advanced (unlimited), Pro (unlimited + 2 hours optimization)

Out-of-scope revisions require upgrade to next tier. Changes beyond tier limits will not proceed until payment adjusted.

10. LIABILITY LIMITATIONS

Morobo liability capped at fees paid in past 12 months (minimum $100). NOT liable for indirect damages, third-party failures, or AI errors.

11. DATA & PRIVACY

Client data retained while service active. Upon termination, Client can request export within 30 days. Permanent deletion 30 days after final billing.

12. AI AGENT SPECIFIC TERMS

Client acknowledges AI may produce inaccurate outputs. Client assumes all risk. Non-Compete: During active contract, Client cannot build competing AI agents.

13. APP-SPECIFIC: REVENUE SHARE

Morobo retains 1% of gross subscription revenue (only if app earns >$2,500/month). IN ADDITION to monthly hosting fee.

14. TERMINATION & CONSEQUENCES

Client may cancel with 30 days notice. Service continues until notice period ends. Domain/app archived, data deleted, access revoked.

15. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Disputes resolved: Negotiation ‚Üí Mediation ‚Üí Binding Arbitration in Travis County, Texas per AAA Commercial Arbitration Rules.

16. GENERAL TERMS

Entire agreement includes MSA + SOW + DPA. Morobo may update terms with 30 days notice. Non-assignable without consent.

Privacy Policy & Data Handling

Data We Collect

Website Visitors: IP, browser type, pages visited, contact form submissions

App Users: Email, username, usage data, device info, location (if permitted)

AI Agent Clients: Account credentials, config data, usage logs, end-user messages

Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract: Processing necessary for service delivery
  • Consent: User explicit agreement (opt-in for emails)
  • Legitimate Interest: Analytics, security, fraud prevention
  • Legal Obligation: Tax records, law enforcement

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve services
  • Respond to inquiries
  • Prevent fraud and security threats
  • Billing and subscription management
  • Enhance AI models (aggregated, anonymized data)

Data Retention

Website Contact Forms: 1 year or until inquiry resolved

App Users: Active + 30 days after deletion

AI Agent Clients: While customer; 30 days after cancellation

Usage Logs: 90 days

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data. Email privacy@morobo.org. We respond within 30 days.

Data Security

HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 at rest, regular audits, access controls, employee NDAs.

Third-Party Sharing

We share data with Stripe, Supabase, AWS, Google Analytics, OpenAI, RevenueCat. These parties have their own privacy policies. We do NOT sell data to advertisers.
