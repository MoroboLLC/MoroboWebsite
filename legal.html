Terms of Service ‚Äî Master Services Agreement

Scope

This document governs the relationship between Morobo and any client for website development, app development, AI agent creation, hosting, integrations, and dashboard services.

Key Sections

  • Services and deliverables definition
  • Client responsibilities and approvals
  • Change request process and fees
  • Payment schedule and milestones
  • Hosting, maintenance, and monitoring terms
  • Data handling and security obligations
  • Termination rights and post-termination obligations
  • Governing law and arbitration clauses

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Always attach a project-specific SOW and an Order Form to this agreement before client signature.

Privacy Policy & Data Handling

Explains how we collect, use, store, and share personal data for websites, apps, and AI agents.

Coverage

  • Categories of data collected (contact, account, payment, logs)
  • Purposes of processing and legal bases
  • Third-party processors and integrations (Stripe, Supabase, etc.)
  • User rights (access, correction, deletion, portability)
  • Data retention and deletion policy
  • Security measures and incident response
  • International transfers and safeguards

Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Use this addendum when processing personal data on behalf of a client.

Key Elements

  1. Nature and purpose of processing
  2. Duration and categories of data
  3. Subprocessors and change notification
  4. Security measures and certifications
  5. Data subject rights assistance
  6. Return or deletion of data on termination
  7. Breach notification (72 hours)

End-User License Agreement (EULA)

For delivered software (mobile apps or desktop tools) distributed to end users.

Covers

  • License grant and scope
  • Restrictions on use
  • Updates and support terms
  • Warranty disclaimers
  • Termination and revocation

Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Define uptime guarantees, maintenance windows, and incident response times for hosting and dashboard services.

Typical Commitments

  • 99.9% uptime for production environments
  • Scheduled maintenance windows (2am‚Äì4am UTC)
  • Incident response within 1 hour
  • Credits for service lapses

Acceptable Use & Security Policy

Key Policies

  • Prohibited content and activities (illegal, infringing, abusive)
  • Account security responsibilities
  • MFA (multi-factor authentication) recommendations
  • Incident reporting and cooperation

Payments, Billing & Refunds

Standard payment terms, accepted methods, overdue handling, and refund policy.

Coverage

  • Payment methods (Stripe, bank transfer, etc.)
  • Invoicing and payment terms (Net 30, etc.)
  • Overdue payment and suspension policy
  • Refund and chargeback handling
  • Tax responsibilities
  • Recurring billing and cancellation

Clarifies who owns code, design assets, data, and third-party components.

Key Provisions

Liability, Indemnity & Dispute Resolution

Standard limitations on liability and dispute resolution approach.

Key Clauses

  • Exclusions for indirect/consequential damages

Statement of Work (SOW) Template

Project-specific attachment to the Master Services Agreement. Complete one per project.

SOW Sections

  • Project summary and objectives
  • Deliverables and acceptance criteria
  • Timeline, milestones, and dependencies
  • Payment schedule (deposits, milestones, final)
  • Resources, team roles, and responsibilities
  • Change control process
  • Termination and liability terms

Ready-to-use form templates for client signature. Download, print, or embed in your onboarding flow.
